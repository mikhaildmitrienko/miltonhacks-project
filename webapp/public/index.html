A New Way to Eat Together

    Have you ever struggled to pick a restaurant at which to meet? Are you frequently unable to make dining decisions for you and your friends? Date Maker chooses the closest, high quality restaurants to you and your friends, eliminating the need for argument and indecision in times of hunger. Just enter the type of restaurant you feel like eating at and with whom you want to go and Date Maker does the rest! If you can't decide that either, Date Maker can pick randomly based on you and your friend's personal preferences!
